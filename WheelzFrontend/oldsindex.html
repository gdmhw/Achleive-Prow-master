About Wheelz

About Us
We are AP Systems; a group of students from Heriot-Watt University who design bespoke systems. We created Wheelz to make bike sharing easy. Please browse this website as you please and download our app to book a bike!

How to Book?
Our App is the easiest way to book!

We know our customers want to book their bikes at the click of their fingers. That's why we made an App! Always on your phone, never more than a couple taps away from booking your first bike with us. Please download it on the Play Store via the button located below!
